Ho Chi Minh

Ho Chi Minh was a political leader who spearheaded the communist movement in Vietnam. He spent some time in the U.S, the U.K, China, Russia, and france. He lead North Vietnam for years on end before eventually deciding to invade South Vietnam. His legacy is still celebrated today within Vietnam.
