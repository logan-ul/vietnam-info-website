Things to do in Vietnam as a Tourist:

Cruise Through Ha Long Bay

Tourists can take boat cruises through the limestone islands and emerald waters of Ha Long Bay. Popular activities include kayaking, cave exploration, swimming, and overnight cruises.

Explore the Ancient Town of Hoi An

Visitors can walk through the lantern-lit streets of Hoi An, visit historic buildings, shop at local markets, and try traditional Vietnamese food. The city is known for its preserved architecture and cultural history.

Visit the Cu Chi Tunnels

At Cu Chi Tunnels, tourists can learn about the underground tunnel systems used during the Vietnam War and even walk through sections of the tunnels.

Relax on Phu Quoc Island

Phu Quoc Island is known for beaches, snorkeling, scuba diving, seafood markets, and tropical resorts. It is one of Vietnam’s top beach destinations.

Visit the Imperial City of Hue

Travelers can tour the historic palaces, temples, and gates inside the Imperial City of Hue, which was once the capital of the Nguyen Dynasty.
